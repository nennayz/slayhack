# Age Like Fine Wine - Tracking Plan Draft

Status: review_ready
Owner: Roxy Rise
Audience: women 35-44
Boundary: measurement plan only; checkout, live ads, public sales, and live publishing remain locked until Captain approval.

## Purpose

This plan defines what must be measured before and after the Age Like Fine Wine pilot launch. It keeps launch review practical: traffic, conversion, delivery, support, and learning all need proof before the offer is scaled.

## Primary Questions

1. Are women 35-44 clicking into the offer from the lead magnet and content push?
2. Does the sales page make the promise clear enough to reach checkout?
3. Does checkout copy reduce hesitation without overpromising?
4. Does delivery work cleanly after purchase?
5. Do buyers understand the first action after download?
6. Which content angle creates the strongest buying signal?

## Launch Metrics

Traffic:
- Sales page visits
- Lead magnet bridge clicks
- Content push link clicks
- Traffic source by day

Conversion:
- Sales page to checkout click rate
- Checkout start count
- Completed purchase count
- Order bump acceptance count, if the bump is approved later

Delivery:
- Successful download count
- Delivery email open rate
- Delivery email click rate
- Support tickets related to access or download

Customer signal:
- Reply rate to delivery email
- Refund requests, once refund terms are approved
- Most mentioned chapter, routine, or checklist
- Questions that reveal confusion in the promise or instructions

Content learning:
- Best hook by clicks
- Best creative by saves or shares
- Best CTA by click-through
- Content that attracts the wrong audience

## 24-Hour Review

Check:
- Sales page visits
- Checkout clicks
- Purchase count
- Delivery issues
- Support requests
- Highest-click content post

Decision:
- If traffic is low, improve content distribution before changing the offer.
- If traffic is present but checkout clicks are weak, review headline, mockup, promise, and price framing.
- If checkout starts but purchases are weak, review checkout copy, trust cues, payment friction, and refund terms.
- If delivery issues appear, pause scaling until fulfillment is fixed.

## 72-Hour Review

Check:
- Conversion trend by traffic source
- Most effective content angle
- Buyer questions and support friction
- Refund or dissatisfaction signal
- Whether the free lead magnet is attracting the right reader

Decision:
- Continue manual launch if signals are clean.
- Revise sales page if click-through is weak.
- Revise delivery and start-here instructions if buyers ask avoidable setup questions.
- Do not scale paid traffic until delivery and support are stable.

## Dashboard Recording

Before launch:
- Confirm tracking events are defined.
- Confirm checkout remains locked until Captain approval.
- Confirm delivery links are testable.
- Confirm support inbox and refund terms are approved.

After launch:
- Record 24-hour proof.
- Record 72-hour proof.
- Add a learning note to the daily learning loop.
- Decide whether to revise, continue, bundle, or pause.

## Review Checklist

- Metrics answer launch decisions, not vanity reporting.
- Delivery and support are measured, not only sales.
- Content learning ties back to the 35-44 audience.
- Event names are stable enough for later dashboard automation.
- No live checkout or public publish is implied by this draft.

## Approval Gate

This draft is ready for review, but it does not approve checkout activation, live sales, live ads, platform publishing, or automatic tracking integrations.
