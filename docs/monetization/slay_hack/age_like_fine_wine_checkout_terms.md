# Age Like Fine Wine - Checkout Terms Draft

Status: review_ready
Boundary: Draft checkout policy only. No payment link, public checkout, live sale, live publish, paid traffic, or automatic fulfillment is active.

## Product

Age Like Fine Wine is a digital PDF guide for women 35-44 who want practical beauty, confidence, and routine guidance. It is not a physical product.

## Delivery

- Delivery method: secure digital download link after purchase.
- Backup delivery: purchase confirmation email with the approved download link.
- Target delivery timing: immediate after checkout completion once fulfillment is connected and tested.
- Required preflight: test purchase, receipt email, download link, mobile PDF opening, desktop PDF opening, and failed-download support path.

## Support

- Support channel: approved support inbox listed on the receipt and delivery email.
- Support scope: download access, missing receipt, duplicate charge questions, file opening issues, and basic order questions.
- Support response target: within 2 business days.
- Social DMs should not be the primary support channel.

## Refund Draft

Recommended low-risk digital-product policy:

If a buyer cannot access the file and support cannot resolve the issue, refund the purchase. If the buyer accidentally purchases twice, refund the duplicate order. Because this is a digital download, general change-of-mind refunds are not active unless Nayz approves that policy before checkout activation.

## Buyer Expectations

- The guide provides general beauty education and routine planning.
- The guide does not provide medical, dermatology, supplement, or diagnosis advice.
- Results vary by person.
- Buyers should patch test products, follow product labels, and consult a qualified professional for medical skin concerns or prescription-product questions.
