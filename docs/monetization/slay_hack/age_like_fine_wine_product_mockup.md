# Age Like Fine Wine - Product Mockup Draft

Status: review_ready
Owner: Lila Lens
Audience: women 35-44
Boundary: review asset only; checkout and public sales remain locked until Captain approval.

## Mockup Purpose

Create one premium product image set that makes Age Like Fine Wine feel like a polished paid e-book, not a generic PDF download. The mockup should support the sales page hero, checkout thumbnail, launch post visuals, and delivery confirmation.

## Visual Direction

- Product feel: premium beauty, mature confidence, clean routine, modern but not teenage.
- Reader signal: women 35-44 who want elevated routines for real skin, real schedules, and real confidence.
- Brand fit: SlayHack voice, character-led guidance, practical glow-up energy, direct and polished.
- Avoid: medical claims, anti-aging shame, heavy clinical styling, teen-trend visuals, cluttered Canva templates.

## Required Mockup Set

1. Sales page hero mockup
   - 3D e-book cover angled slightly left.
   - Subtle page stack or tablet frame behind it.
   - Copy-safe empty space on the right for headline and CTA.

2. Checkout thumbnail
   - Square crop that still reads clearly at small size.
   - Title and premium beauty signal must remain legible.
   - No busy background texture.

3. Social launch crop
   - 4:5 portrait crop for Instagram and Facebook.
   - Mockup centered with soft product shadow.
   - Leave top/bottom breathing room for caption overlay if needed.

4. Delivery confirmation image
   - Clean flat-lay or tablet view.
   - Should feel like "your guide is ready" rather than an ad.

## Cover Treatment

- Title: Age Like Fine Wine
- Subtitle direction: Beauty, confidence, and glow-up routines for 35+
- Visual anchor: elegant mature beauty routine, skincare, soft cosmetic tools, refined glow.
- Character integration: optional small tip-card stack from Auntie V, Bella Mist, Sera, Sunny, Wanda, and Conceal; do not crowd the cover.
- Palette direction: warm rose, wine, champagne, deep plum accent, clean ivory space.

## Production Prompt

Create a premium e-book product mockup for "Age Like Fine Wine", a beauty and confidence PDF guide for women 35-44. Show a polished 3D e-book cover and subtle tablet/page stack, with warm rose, wine, champagne, ivory, and deep plum accents. The mood is mature, elegant, practical, and modern, not teenage and not clinical. Include beauty routine cues such as skincare, foundation, SPF, and soft cosmetic tools without clutter. Keep the product title legible. Leave clean copy-safe space for a sales page headline. No medical claims, no anti-aging shame, no before-after framing.

## Review Checklist

- The title is legible on desktop and mobile crops.
- The mockup immediately reads as a paid e-book product.
- The visual tone matches women 35-44, not teens or clinical skincare ads.
- The cover does not imply medical, dermatology, or guaranteed outcome claims.
- Sales page, checkout, social, and delivery crops can come from the same direction.
- Final image files must be compressed before PDF or dashboard embedding.

## Approval Gate

This draft can be used to create or review the visual mockup, but it does not approve checkout activation, public launch, or live posting.
