# Stadium Sweethearts Project Brief

Stadium Sweethearts is an AI-only sports fan-cam entertainment page. The brand
creates short-form vertical clips and images that feel like a cinematic stadium
camera naturally found the sweetheart of the night.

## Core Position

- Project type: AI-generated short-form social media page.
- Format: vertical short videos, fan-cam style, stadium atmosphere.
- Audience: men in the United States, mainly ages 21-44.
- Brand sentence: The camera found the sweetheart of the night.
- Tagline: AI Fan-Cam Moments.
- Campaign phrase: The Camera Found Her.

## PM Profile

The project PM is Stadium, a male sporty showrunner for the page. He should feel
like a sideline producer and creative coach who knows how to package game-day
moments for short-form platforms.

- Role: Project Manager and fan-cam game-plan lead.
- Personality: confident, athletic, direct, playful, protective, non-crude.
- Look: polished sports-lifestyle fit, varsity or bomber jacket, headset,
  stadium pass, sneakers, and clean navy/pink accents.
- Signature props: navy playbook, pink whistle lanyard, replay monitor glow,
  and stadium media credential.
- Creative taste: face and emotion first, crowd energy second, outfit third.
- Language: calls ideas "plays," batches "game plans," and winners "replayable."
- Responsibility: keep every post fictional, adult 21+, tasteful, AI-disclosed,
  and free from real team marks or real-person resemblance.

## Brand Rules

- All characters are fictional AI-generated adults 21+.
- Do not use real person, celebrity, influencer, athlete, girlfriend, wife, or
  public-figure likenesses.
- Do not use real team logos, official marks, or rights-protected branding unless
  rights are secured.
- Do not imply the content is real footage.
- Do not frame the content as secret filming, hidden-camera, or voyeuristic.
- Do not use minors, teen-coded styling, nudity, lingerie, explicit poses, or
  explicit body-focus framing.
- Required disclosure: All Stadium Sweethearts characters are AI-generated
  fictional adults 21+. No real person likeness is used.

## Visual Identity

- Stadium Pink: logo accents, hearts, captions, glow.
- Night Navy: stadium-night background.
- Bright White: text and contrast.
- Jersey Red: sports energy and football visuals.
- Golden Light: sunset, tailgate, and cinematic glow.

Visual balance should be about 60% stadium, 25% sweetheart identity, and 15%
flirty glam. The best creative formula is face and emotion first, outfit second,
body third.

## Content Pillars

- Fan-Cam Crush: the stadium camera finds the cutest fan of the night.
- Game-Day Fit: jersey, cap, denim, team-color styling, and fit-check energy.
- Stadium Reaction: touchdown, shot, home run, overtime, or scoreboard emotion.
- Rival Energy: playful opposing colors without real team marks.
- Sweetheart of the Night: hero smile, celebration, or fan-cam reaction.

## Clip Structure

Use 6-8 second vertical loops:

1. Broadcast zoom from crowd or stadium seats.
2. Character watches the game, laughs, cheers, or reacts.
3. She notices the camera and gives a playful smile or look.
4. Crowd, score, scoreboard, or friend-group energy reinforces the moment.
5. Return to cheering or a loopable ending.

Every post needs a moment. Static beauty shots are not enough.

## Safe Caption Examples

- The camera found her.
- Game-day crush energy.
- That smile deserved a replay.
- Wrong team. Right energy.
- Stadium lights hit different.
- The crowd-cam chose correctly.
- Sweetheart of the night.
- Replay that reaction.

Avoid captions like "she had no idea we were watching," "caught her secretly,"
"zoom in on her," or body-focused language.
